Theory of Change

The project contributes to this longer-term impact, with the development and start-up of a local knowledge & competence training center and innovation hub. Working at a competence center and innovation hub means we are able to build a vibrant roadmap for the future!

We start by analyzing the current situation of the HEI that will intend to transform to a knowledge & competence training center for AI & IoT and their local industry and will tranfer their knowledge into an innovation hub. Further the local industry will be analyzed in terms of applicability of internships and technical staff trainings. Short intensive courses about AI and IoT will be organised in the competence center.

These actions will lead to a plan of approach for the local partner that contains information about planned training sessions, laboratories to be installed, set up of multidisciplinary student projects and internships, and the setup of the innovation hub where professionals will have to possibility to experience in the lab and get coached by well-educated AI & IoT engineers. In the hub the professional field will have incentives and equipment to where ideas can germinate and get tested before they can be scaled up of started throughout the company.

what is an innovation hub

So the innovation hub is the connection and collaboration tool between the professional and the academic field. It has the focus on developing new technologies and platforms with client experience in mind, to create financial well-being for more people. Dedicated staff will attract professionals to the hub. By design, an innovation hub fosters a spirit of imagination and collaboration. Often, great ideas come from unexpected sources. To be able to connect the professional field with an academic field feeds entrepreneurship. The project aims at an increase in employability, in added value produced locally, sustained life-ling-learning and long-term high-quality knowledge gain in technology. With the implemantation of our competence center in I(O)T and innovation hub we increase the number of people with relevant practical skills for employment and decent jobs, financial success and enterpreneurship.
